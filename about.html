Hello!

My name is Mervin! I’m a passionate, dependable designer and I focus primarily on developing products and websites that make life easier for customers and users. I’m a problem solver with experience in desktop publishing, graphic design, and email marketing. In my spare time, which is rare, I enjoy woodworking. Even if the projects end up in the fire pit, I love honing my skills and learning about new techniques.
